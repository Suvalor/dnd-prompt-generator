# AdSense SEO MVP Keyword Research

Date: 2026-06-01
Goal: Find non-head, medium-demand keywords suitable for a free tool website that can be built as a 1-2 day MVP and monetized with Google AdSense.

## Research Basis

Sources checked:

- Google Trends AI Search Trends report for rising AI search behavior.
- Google Trends / keyword trend tooling references.
- Public keyword research pages with directional monthly search volume, competition, CPC, and trend indicators.
- Current examples of free tool sites in calculator, SEO, creator, and AI utility categories.

Important caveat:

- Google Trends gives relative interest, not exact search volume.
- Third-party keyword volume estimates are directional, not truth.
- Before building, each selected keyword should be checked again in Google Trends and Google SERP manually.

## Selection Criteria

Good candidates should:

- Solve a narrow, immediate pain.
- Be useful without login.
- Work as a free tool, not just an article.
- Avoid head terms like "AI generator", "calculator", "resume builder", "SEO tool".
- Support long-tail SEO pages.
- Be buildable in 1-2 days.
- Be AdSense-safe: not adult, gambling, illegal, copyrighted-download, or dangerous medical/financial advice.

## Top Recommendations

### 1. DND Character Prompt Generator / DND Token Prompt Tool

Primary keyword cluster:

- dnd image generator
- dnd character image generator
- dnd character portrait generator
- dnd token maker
- dnd fantasy character prompt generator

Trend signal:

- Google Trends' AI Search Trends report says AI image generation is at an all-time high and specifically notes that "dnd image generator" spiked this year.

Why this is attractive:

- Very specific user pain: players and dungeon masters need character portraits, NPCs, monsters, scenes, and tokens.
- Avoids the huge generic "AI image generator" head term.
- Can be launched without expensive image generation on day 1: generate detailed prompts, token specs, scene descriptions, and copyable image prompts for Midjourney, ChatGPT, Gemini, Leonardo, etc.
- Strong SEO expansion path: race pages, class pages, monster pages, scene pages.

1-2 day MVP:

- Form inputs: race, class, gender, age, alignment, mood, armor, weapon, art style, background, output type.
- Outputs:
  - Character image prompt.
  - Token prompt.
  - Portrait prompt.
  - Backstory seed.
  - Negative prompt.
- Add 30 static SEO pages:
  - "Tiefling Warlock Prompt Generator"
  - "Elf Ranger Portrait Prompt"
  - "DND Tavern Scene Prompt"
  - "DND Monster Prompt Generator"

AdSense fit:

- Good. Entertainment/hobby content is generally safer than finance/medical/legal.

Risk:

- Competition exists, but most competitors focus on image generation. A lightweight prompt/tool site can rank for long-tail combinations.

Verdict:

- Best first MVP.

### 2. AI Interior Design Prompt Generator

Primary keyword cluster:

- best ai for interior design
- ai interior design prompt
- ai room design prompt
- living room ai design prompt
- bedroom interior design ai prompt
- room style prompt generator

Trend signal:

- Google Trends' AI Search Trends report compares "best ai for vacation planning" vs "best ai for interior design", and public commentary around the report notes strong interest in AI interior design.

Why this is attractive:

- Very concrete pain: user wants to redesign a room and needs a good prompt or style direction.
- Medium/high commercial intent: home decor, furniture, design, renovation.
- Can be launched without image generation first.
- Good content expansion: room type + style + color + budget pages.

1-2 day MVP:

- Form inputs: room type, style, current problem, color preference, budget, room size, natural light, must-keep furniture.
- Outputs:
  - AI image prompt.
  - Shopping keywords.
  - Color palette.
  - Layout advice.
  - Before/after prompt variants.
- SEO pages:
  - "Small Bedroom AI Interior Design Prompt"
  - "Japandi Living Room Prompt"
  - "Rental Apartment Makeover Prompt"
  - "Home Office AI Design Prompt"

AdSense fit:

- Good. Home improvement advertisers tend to be monetizable.

Risk:

- Upload-photo AI design tools are stronger products. MVP should avoid competing head-on and focus on prompt + style planning.

Verdict:

- Strong second choice.

### 3. AI Tattoo Idea Prompt Generator

Primary keyword cluster:

- ai tattoo generator
- tattoo prompt generator
- minimalist tattoo idea generator
- forearm tattoo prompt
- cyber sigilism tattoo ideas

Trend signal:

- AI image generator demand is high, and many current sites are already targeting AI tattoo generation. The keyword family has clear user intent.

Why this is attractive:

- Immediate pain: "I want a tattoo idea but cannot describe it well."
- Long-tail pages are easy: style + placement + subject.
- Easy MVP: prompt generation, style glossary, placement guide, idea variations.

1-2 day MVP:

- Inputs: subject, placement, style, size, meaning, gender preference, detail level.
- Outputs:
  - Tattoo prompt.
  - 5 design variations.
  - Artist brief.
  - Placement notes.
  - Avoid list.
- SEO pages:
  - "Minimalist Forearm Tattoo Prompt"
  - "Cyber Sigilism Tattoo Idea Generator"
  - "Fine Line Flower Tattoo Prompt"

AdSense fit:

- Generally okay, but keep content clean and avoid adult/explicit/body-shaming content.

Risk:

- Many competitors already exist.
- Image generation can become cost-heavy if offered free.

Verdict:

- Good, but I would build after DND or interior design.

### 4. Keyword Golden Ratio Calculator For Bloggers

Primary keyword cluster:

- kgr calculator
- keyword golden ratio calculator
- low competition keyword calculator
- allintitle keyword ratio

Trend / market signal:

- Tool-site trend boards recommend root terms like generator, calculator, and converter for discoverable utility sites. Existing KGR calculator pages show clear SEO use cases.

Why this is attractive:

- Directly helps SEO users.
- Extremely easy to build.
- Can support your own future AdSense/SEO workflow.
- Strong article/tool hybrid potential.

1-2 day MVP:

- Input keyword, monthly volume, allintitle count.
- Output KGR score, difficulty label, recommendation.
- Batch CSV input.
- Export CSV.
- Add guide pages for KGR examples.

AdSense fit:

- Good, but traffic may be mostly marketers with ad blockers.

Risk:

- Smaller audience than AI creative tools.

Verdict:

- Best "utility for SEO people"; less viral than DND/interior.

### 5. AI SEO Title And Meta Description Generator

Primary keyword cluster:

- ai seo tool
- seo title generator
- meta description generator
- blog title generator
- article title generator

Search signal:

- Public keyword data shows "ai seo tool" around 5.4K monthly searches, low competition in one dataset, and positive trend. "article title generator" appears lower-volume but lower competition.

Why this is attractive:

- Very easy to build.
- Useful for bloggers, small businesses, and marketers.
- Can generate many long-tail pages by platform and use case.

1-2 day MVP:

- Input: topic, keyword, audience, tone.
- Output:
  - 10 SEO titles.
  - 10 meta descriptions.
  - URL slug.
  - H1/H2 outline.
  - SERP preview.

AdSense fit:

- Good.

Risk:

- Crowded category.
- Hard to differentiate unless you niche down: "Local SEO Title Generator", "YouTube Blog Title Generator", "SaaS Landing Page Meta Generator".

Verdict:

- Build only if niched down.

### 6. Terms And Conditions Generator For Micro SaaS

Primary keyword cluster:

- terms and conditions generator
- terms generator for saas
- website terms generator
- app terms and conditions generator

Search signal:

- Public keyword data shows "terms and conditions generator" around 9.9K monthly searches, low competition, and transactional intent.

Why this is attractive:

- Clear pain.
- Good monetization intent.
- Easy form-based MVP.

AdSense fit:

- Caution. This is legal-adjacent YMYL content. It needs disclaimers and should avoid pretending to be legal advice.

Risk:

- Legal reliability risk.
- Trust burden is higher than entertainment/tool niches.

Verdict:

- Not ideal for first AdSense site. Possible later with careful disclaimers.

### 7. AdSense RPM / Revenue Calculator

Primary keyword cluster:

- adsense rpm calculator
- adsense revenue calculator
- website ad revenue calculator
- page rpm calculator

Why this is attractive:

- Directly aligned with your own goal.
- Simple calculator.
- Can generate useful articles around traffic, RPM, CTR, CPC.

1-2 day MVP:

- Inputs: pageviews, CTR, CPC or RPM, niche, country.
- Outputs:
  - Estimated daily/monthly/yearly revenue.
  - Required traffic for target income.
  - RPM scenarios by niche.

AdSense fit:

- Good, but likely competitive.

Risk:

- May attract site owners rather than mass consumer traffic.

Verdict:

- Good supporting tool, not best first main site.
